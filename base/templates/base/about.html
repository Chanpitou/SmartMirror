About

Goal

Our project aims to infuse a normal mirror with technology like real time updates such as news, weather, and calendar events. These are the fundamental functionalities we want to achieve to call it a smart mirror, marking our baseline. To cross the limit of conventional functionalities available in the market, we plan to develop a website for the user to personalize their experience as well as introduce a face recognition system. The vision will be achieved by programming GUIs (Graphical User Interface) with the help of python’s tkinter library, website with CSS and HTML and managing our databases on our server. Project’s core will include Raspberry Pi 4 to power our system, and PCB which to connect sound systems, Raspberry PI and lights. Encased in a sleek wooden frame, the user will have a streamlines experience with only the mirror and website curated by the developers. This approach aims to heighten user experience by giving a minimalistic view while reducing their risks of injury.
